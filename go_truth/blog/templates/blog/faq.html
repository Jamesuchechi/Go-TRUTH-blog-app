Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to the Go Truth FAQ section! Here, we’ve compiled a list of the most common questions from our community to help you navigate our platform with ease. If your question isn’t answered here, don’t hesitate to contact us via our Contact Us page.

General Questions

What is Go Truth?

Go Truth is a platform dedicated to delivering accurate, reliable information to users worldwide, created by Okpara James Uchechi to foster a community of truth-seekers.

How do I sign up?

Visit the homepage, click 'Create Post' or 'Profile', and follow the registration prompts to join our community.

Technical Support

Why can’t I post content?

Ensure you’re logged in and have the necessary permissions. Check file sizes for images/videos and contact support if issues persist.

How do I report a problem?

Use the 'Contact Us' page to submit a detailed report, and our team will assist you promptly.
